About

Aminnovate is a 48-hour software and hardware hackathon.

Aminnovate will host a weekend of fun, learning and innovation whilst letting students enjoy the city of joy. It will act as a bridge between theory and practical and will also give the opportunity to nurture fresh ideas and create projects with significant socio-economic impact on our society which has never happened till now.

For Code of Conduct, we will be following Devfolio's Code of Conduct.
